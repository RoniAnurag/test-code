The Genesis

"I Left the 99 for the One"

In 2009, a missionary in South India cried out to God after walking all day to preach to only five people. That night, a vision shook his world. The Holy Spirit whispered: "The World Shall Be Saved."

Today, GST Global is a fire burning across nations, reaching the unreached with the uncompromised Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Founder

K. Daniel is the founder of GOD SOLDIERS TEAM GLOBAL. He is an evangelist who has spent most of his ministry preaching the gospel in more than 25 states in India and globally. As a missionary, Daniel has led thousands of people to Christ face-to-face through open-air evangelistic campaigns in some of the most dangerous, difficult, and remote locations on earth.

"God is working to create a community of redeemed people from every tribe, tongue and nation for His glory."
