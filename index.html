Meet The General

Our Founder

K.Daniel is the founder of GOD SOLDIERS TEAM GLOBAL. He is an evangelist who has spent most of his ministry preaching the gospel in more than 25 states in India and globally. As a missionary, Daniel has led thousands of people to Christ face-to-face through open-air evangelistic campaigns in some of the most dangerous and remote locations on earth.

“God is working to create a community of redeemed people from every tribe, tongue and nation for his glory. This will help you better understand of God's mission (missio-Dei) and the vital role of Christ Followers is called to play in advancing his kingdom.”
